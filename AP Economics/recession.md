# recession

-  month period of decline in real [[gross domestic product|GDP]]
- really bad recessions are depressions
- leads to
	- more [[unemployment]]
	- decrease in real [[gross domestic product|GDP]]
	- reduced job growth
	- lower interest rates
	- decreasing prices
	- more social problems
