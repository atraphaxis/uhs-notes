# temperament

- inborn traits of a child
	- relatively consistent
	- different from personality
		- not impacted by life experiences
- types
	- easy
		- set times
		- cry but calm easily
		- may sleep through the night
	- difficult
		- cry a lot
		- want to be held consistently
	- slow to warm up
		- shy and apprehensive
