# stress

- process by which we perceive stressors
- different people appraise different situations differently
- can be related to [[fear]]
	- examples
		- public speaking
		- death
- stress response system
	- [[Selye's general adaptation syndrome]]
- caused by
	- catastrophes
	- life changes
	- daily hassles
- can be influenced by personality
	- controversial idea of [[type A personality]] vs [[type B personality]]
- reducing it
	- [[aerobic exercise]]
	- relaxation
	- meditation and [[mindfulness]]
	- social support
	- faith communities
	- healthy living
