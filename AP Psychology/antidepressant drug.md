# antidepressant drug

- used with mood and [[anxiety disorder|anxiety disorders]]
- types
	- selective-[[serotonin]]-[[reuptake]] inhibitors (SSRIs)
- include
	- fluoxetine (Prozac)
	- paxil
- side effects
	- weight gain
	- hypertension
	- dizzy spells
