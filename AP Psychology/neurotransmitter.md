# neurotransmitter

- chemical messenger
	- crosses the synaptic gap in a [[synapse]]
	- bind to receptors on the receiving [[neuron]]
		- lock and key mechanism
	- [[reuptake]]
		- alternatively, after being sent over
			- broken down by enzymes
			- drift away
- includes
	- [[acetylcholine]]
	- [[dopamine]]
	- [[serotonin]]
	- [[norepinephrine]]
	- [[gamma-aminobutyric acid]]
	- [[glutamate]]
	- [[endorphin]]
