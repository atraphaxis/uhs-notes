# taste

- aka gustation
- types
	- sweet
		- energy source
	- sour
		- possibly toxic acid
	- salty
		- sodium essential to biological processes
	- bitter
		- possibly poisonous
	- umami
		- proteins to develop tissue
- using taste buds
	- number decreases with age
		- less sensitivity
	- located on the papillae (bumps on the tongue)
- requires [[sensory interaction]] with [[smell]]
