# post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)

- [[anxiety disorder]] with four or more weeks of the following symptoms following a stressful event
	- haunting memories
	- nightmares
	- social withdrawal
	- jumpy anxiety
	- sleep problems
- can lead to [[post-traumatic growth]]
