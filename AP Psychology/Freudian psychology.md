# Freudian [[psychology]]

- also known as psychoanalytic [[psychology]]
- established by [[Sigmund Freud]]
- emphasized the unconscious and early experiences
- very time consuming
- theorized parts of the mind (iceberg theory)
	- conscious
	- preconscious
	- unconscious
		- repression
