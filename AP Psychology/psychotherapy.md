# psychotherapy

- treatment for [[psychological disorder|psychological disorders]] involving a professional in order to get a person to change their behaviors
- includes
	- [[psychoanalysis]]
	- [[psychodynamic therapy]]
	- [[humanistic therapy]]
	- [[behavior therapy]]
	- [[cognitive therapy]]
	- group therapy
	- family therapy
- desire for [[regression toward the mean]]
